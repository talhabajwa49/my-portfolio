How long until I see results?

SEO is a long-term strategy. Typically, you can expect initial movements within 3 months, with significant traffic and revenue impact between 6-12 months.

Yes, I can work directly with your developers or Content CMS (WordPress, Shopify, Webflow) to implement technical fixes and content updates.

Do you work globally?

Absolutely. I have delivered projects for clients in the USA, Canada, Australia, UK, UAE, and Pakistan, tailoring strategies to local nuances.

Do you do retainers or project-based work?

I offer both. Monthly retainers are best for ongoing growth, while project-based audits or migrations suit specific needs.
